Introducing


NiO

Your personal speech coach!

A cutting-edge speech delivery improvement app designed to help you improve your speech rate, grammar, fluency, and speech emotion!


Equipped with state-of-the-art audio processing algorithms and machine learning models, NiO can become your companion in practicing your speeches to improve your speaking skills and gain confidence in speaking publicly.


Get Started

How to use NiO?

Step 1: Enter your username

Input your username to keep track of your progress while practicing. You can also enter a script by typing in the text box or by uploading a txt file.

Step 2: Record your speech

Talk into your device's mic and start recording your speech.
NiO can handle a maximum of 5 minutes audio.
Tip: Position your mic 6-12 inches away from your mouth for better audio results

Step 3: View your results

After recording your audio, NiO will perform audio processing and analysis of your speech.
It will tell you your score in terms of your speech rate, grammar, and fluency in speaking.
It also tells you what emotion is felt in your way of speaking!
